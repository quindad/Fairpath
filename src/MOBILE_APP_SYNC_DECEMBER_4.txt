1. Offense type & severity (felony/misdemeanor, violent/non-violent)
2. Time since conviction (state-specific waiting periods)
3. State-specific laws (all 50 states)
4. Sentence completion (probation, parole, fines)
5. Additional convictions (multiple charges affect eligibility)
6. Disqualifying factors (sex offenses, violent crimes, pending charges)
7. Alternative options (sealing, pardons if not eligible)
8. Cost & timeline estimates (filing fees, lawyer costs, processing time)
