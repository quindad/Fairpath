### Landlord Rev-Share Program

#### How Landlords Earn
- **Base Rate:** $30 per showing (FLAT)
- **Requirement:** Must rent to at least 1 felon every 45 days

#### Payment Structure
**Option 1: Full Payment (When Compliant)**
- Landlord receives: $30 per showing
- Friend A Felon keeps: Remaining revenue after SingleKey cost ($75 - $18 - $30 = $27)

**Option 2: Reduced Payment (If No Rentals After 45 Days)**
- Landlord receives: $18 per showing (60% of $30)
- FairPath Impact Fund receives: $12 per showing (40% of $30)

#### Payment Release Schedule
- **Every 45 days** (automatic)
- Direct deposit to landlord's account
