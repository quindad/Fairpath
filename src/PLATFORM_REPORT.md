## ğŸ“ˆ WOTC VALUE TIERS (IRS-VERIFIED)

### Tier 1: Base Ex-Felon
**$2,400 per hire**
- Convicted of felony
- Hired within 1 year of release or conviction date
- IRS Form 8850 + ETA Form 9061

### Tier 2: Enhanced Ex-Felon
**$9,000 per hire**
- Ex-felon PLUS one of:
  - **SNAP** (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance)
  - **SSI** (Supplemental Security Income)
  - **TANF** (Temporary Assistance for Needy Families)
  - **EZ** (Empowerment Zone resident)
- Qualifies as "Long-Term Family Assistance Recipient"
- Same IRS forms + proof of assistance

### Tier 3: Qualified Veteran
**$9,600 per hire**
- Veteran who received SNAP for 3+ months
- Unemployed for 4+ weeks (aggregate) in prior year
- Service-connected disabled veteran
- IRS Form 8850 + ETA Form 9061 + DD-214

**Key Insight:**
Many ex-felons qualify for Tier 2 ($9,000) not Tier 1 ($2,400). The calculator lets users see real enhanced value potential.
