4. FAIRPATH LEGAL ğŸŸ£
   Color: Purple (#A855F7)
   Tagline: "Clear Your Record with AI"

   What it is:
   - AI-powered expungement eligibility checker
   - Lawyer marketplace connecting users with attorneys

   How It Works:
   1. User enters criminal charges (offense, year, state)
   2. AI analyzes against all 50-state expungement laws
   3. Instant result: eligible/not eligible + next steps
   4. If eligible, match with verified lawyers in your area

   What the AI Checks:
   - Offense type & severity (felony vs misdemeanor, violent vs non-violent)
   - Time since conviction (waiting periods by state)
   - State-specific laws (all 50 states)
   - Sentence completion (probation, parole, fines)
   - Additional convictions (multiple charges affect eligibility)
   - Disqualifying factors (sex offenses, violent crimes)
   - Alternative options (sealing, pardons if not eligible for expungement)
   - Cost & timeline estimates (filing fees, lawyer costs, processing time)

   Example AI Result:
   "Your 2018 California drug possession conviction is eligible for expungement 
   under PC 1203.4. You completed probation in 2020, so you qualify now. 
   Filing fee: $150. Average timeline: 3-6 months."

   Pricing:
   - 100% FREE for people with criminal records
   - Lawyers pay $20/month for listing + access to pre-qualified leads
   - Users pay lawyer directly for services ($500-2,000 typically)
   - We take NO commission from lawyer fees
